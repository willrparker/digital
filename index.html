About

Personal Experience and Long-Term Goals

My name is William Ronald Parker. I am currently twenty-two years old and have graduated with a law degree. I was born and raised in Ashford, Kent, and have since been very disciplined to achieve my dreams. I believe anything is possible through hard work. And hard work beats talent, when talent fails to work hard.

My work is centred around digital tools to organise information, improve workflows and make complex tasks clearer. I am particularly interested in AI, LegalTech, data protection, privacy processes and structured project development.

I approach projects with a mix of commercial awareness, creative problem-solving and research abilities. My goal is to build a portfolio that showcases practical thinking rather than just interest.

Alongside professional development, I am also interested in writing, creative technology and astrophysics. I see creativity and technical thinking as connected wherein both require structure, judgment and the ability to turn rough ideas into something clear.

Focus AI, LegalTech, data privacy and workflow design.
Approach Practical, organised, research-led and outcome-focused.
Goal To build useful digital systems and develop a career around technology, law and innovation.
